SPACE LAZAGNE

super lasagna that lasts 2,000 years dry or frozen

Tastes like paper machete carbon cheese but has all vitamins + rocks

  • 1 cup of sheep skin
  • 2 tablespoons of herring scales
  • 1/2 tsp of brickle brackle
  • 200 mg black salt
  • 1 jar of eye of newt
  • gluten-free seaweed protein freeze-dried pasta noodles
  • 4 cans of balsamic tomato jam
  1. Throw everything in a blender
  2. Pour it into a plaster mold
  3. Put the mold into a clay oven at 600 degrees for 5 hours
